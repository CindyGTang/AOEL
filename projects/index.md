# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-wrench" %}Research Areas

Our research interests lie in developing, designing and synthesizing new materials for high performance organic electronics. We aim to demonstrate novel and practical applications towards the advancement of organic electronics for next generation devices, particularly in the area of flexible stretchable polymer-based electronics, circuits and sensors. Â We are interested in the design and fundamental understanding of electronic, optoelectronic, mechanical and other properties of electronically and ionically active functional emerging materials. By employing a combination of experimental and theoretical techniques, we aim to uncover and comprehend fundamental phenomena that drive both scientific advancements and technological breakthroughs.
